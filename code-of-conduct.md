# Contributor Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers of this cybersecurity learning repository pledge
to make participation a respectful, constructive, and harassment-free experience for
everyone — regardless of age, background, level of experience, nationality, or identity.

We pledge to act in ways that contribute to an open, welcoming, and ethical community.

---

## Our Standards

### ✅ Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Welcome beginners — everyone starts somewhere
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the learning community
- Show empathy and patience toward other contributors
- Cite sources and give credit where it is due

### ❌ Unacceptable Behaviour

- Harassment, insults, or personal attacks of any kind
- Sharing others' private information without permission
- Publishing or encouraging illegal hacking activity
- Dismissing or belittling others for not knowing something
- Spamming, trolling, or derailing discussions
- Any form of discrimination or exclusion

---

## 🔐 Cybersecurity-Specific Standards

Because this is a security-focused repository, additional standards apply:

| Rule | Reason |
|---|---|
| Do not share real exploits targeting production systems | Prevents real-world harm |
| Do not include credentials, tokens, or live targets | Protects privacy and security |
| Label all intentionally vulnerable examples clearly | Avoids confusion |
| Always pair offense with defense | Promotes a responsible mindset |
| Do not encourage unauthorized access — ever | It is illegal |

Violation of these standards will result in immediate removal of the contribution
and potential blocking from the repository.
